Steve Jobs

1955-2011

"Design is not just what it looks like and feels like, Design is how it works"

Biography

Steve Jobs was an American entrepreneur and co-founder of Apple Inc. Born on February 24, 1955, he played a crucial role in revolutionizing the technology industry. Jobs' relentless pursuit of innovation led to the creation of iconic products like the Macintosh computer, iPod, iPhone, and iPad, which transformed the way we communicate and consume media. His unique vision and attention to detail helped Apple become one of the most valuable companies in the world. Despite facing setbacks, including his temporary departure from Apple, Jobs returned to lead the company to new heights with the launch of the iMac, iTunes, and the App Store.
